Maple-Roasted Carrots

Description

These sweet and stick carrots pack a little heat and are a perfect side for your Thanksgiving dinner.

Keep an eye on them near the end of cooking time as the maple syrup and butter can darken quickly.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. medium carrots
  • 6 tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1⁄3 cup (packed) light brown sugar
  • 1⁄3 cup pure maple syrup
  • 3⁄4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
  • Kosher salt
  • Flaky sea salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°. Line a rimmed baking sheet with 3 sheets of foil (you'll need it!). Cut carrots on a diagonal into 3" pieces.
  2. Spread out carrots on foil. Evenly top with butter, brown sugar, syrup, and red pepper flakes. Season with kosher salt and toss to combine.
  3. Bake carrots, tossing halfway through, until tender and browned around the edges, 30 minutes. Transfer to a platter (with syrup, if desired). Sprinkle with sea salt (if using)
