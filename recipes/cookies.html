Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These chocolate chip cookies are thick, gooey and delicious.

They take no time to make and are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Baking Soda

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Melt butter until liquid but not hot.
  2. Combine white and brown sugar with melted butter. Whisk together egg and vanilla extract and add to sugar and butter mixture. Add dry ingredients to wet. Add in chocolate chips.
  3. Bake for 7-10 mins.
